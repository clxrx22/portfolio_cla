About Me

To begin I welcome you in my portfolio !
My name is Clara and I'm 20 years old and I'm in DN MADe digital at Gobelins, Paris.
I'm a curious, open-minded, joyful, creative, serious and committed person; it seems that I'm also a bit too stressed and too organized. (But it seems...)

Commitments

A very committed citizen, I was a member of the BDE (where I was in charge of social networks and partnerships). I was also president of the Maison Des Lycéens (MDL) at the Lycée René Cassin in Arpajon (91), a member of the Conseil Académique de Vie Lycéenne (CAVL), and an eco-delegate.
These commitments are important to me.

I'm particularly attached to freedom, women's rights, children's rights and the lgbtqia2+ cause.
In my spare time, I'm also a member of the board of my archery club, responsible for children's activities.

Material

I use two types of material for my creations.
Firstly, for paper, I use: pencils of different sizes, black pens of different widths, colored pencils, watercolors, pastels from Campus, Promarker, Brushmarker and POSCA. I also use an Ipad for digital drawings.

To start designing and creating websites, I use Figma, the Adobe Suite and HTML, CSS, SCSS, JS and Type.js .
