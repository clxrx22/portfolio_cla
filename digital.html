Fruity Project

The goal of the Fruity app is to reconnect people
with food and healthy eating.

We have seen a clear deterioration in nutrition over
the past few years.
People are used to eating in an unhealthy way with
fast food and inappropriate products.
They don't know how to use bouncy products
like fruits.

Fruity is here to solve this problem by giving
people a tool to use fruit properly in all situations.

In a first step we have
take the most popular
and most used fruits on the
market in order to target
as many
people possible.
We then collected data and
specificities on them.

These data are present
to guide people and inform them about
the product they are using. Then, we searched for
recipes on all categories (dish, dessert, drinks,...)
in order to offer a lot of products to people and
to show the diversity that Fruity gives. Finally,
we added features and gave the overall look of the
application by designing it

The coding aspect of the project was a whole other part, it allowed us to surpass ourselves and to improve our level This project gave us a real vision of the professional life. Thanks to this project we could see all the process of creation and contribution of ideas to arrive a concrete product
