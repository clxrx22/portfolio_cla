Hello :)

To begin I welcome you in my
portfolio !

My name is Clara and I'm 18
years old and I'm in DN MADe
digital at Gobelins, Paris.

I'm a curious, open-minded,
joyful, creative, serious and
committed person; it seems
that I'm also a bit too
stressed and too organized.
(But it seems...)

My commitments

I am a very committed citizen,
I am currently a member of the
BDE (where I take care of
social networks and
partnerships). The previous
year I was President of the
Maison Des Lycéens (MDL) of
the René Cassin high school
located in Arpajon (91),
member of the Conseil
Académique de Vie Lycéenne
(CAVL), and eco-delegate for 2
years.

These commitments are
important to me and I do my
best to be a good elected
official.

I have a particular commitment
to freedom, women's rights,
children's rights and the
lgbtqia2+ cause.

In my spare time I am also
involved as a member of the
board of my archery club as a
children's representative.

My material

For my creations I use 2 types
of setup.

First of all for paper or

I use: pencils of different

brand Campus, Promarker,

To start designing and
creating websites I use
Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator,
