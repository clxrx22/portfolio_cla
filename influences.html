Dan Kitchener

These works are by Dan Kitchener, a street artist from London. His works represent technology and Japanese culture.
His work fascinates me, he plays a lot with colors and lights and he also gives movement to his works. Moreover, he works on several supports (walls, canvas, tablet etc...)

Brunograffer

Works by Brunograffer, a tiktokker from Strasbourg. His work is original and fun! He likes everything colorful and draws on different supports. He also likes to customize shoes, cards, tickets, shells etc...)

Bublegumsr

Works of Bublegumsr, an original and realistic street artist. In his works we can find animals and people more or less known.
