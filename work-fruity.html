Fruity

The goal of the Fruity application is to reconnect people with food and healthy eating. We have seen a clear deterioration in nutrition over the past the last few years.

People are used to eating unhealthy food with fast food and not knowing how to using bouncy products like fruit.

Fruity is here to solve this problem by giving people the tools to use fruit correctly in every situation.
