Steps of customization


1. Draw the stencil on a large piece of
cardboard (that fits the size of the t-shirt).


2. Cut out the stencil properly without using the
wrong piece.


3. Once finished, place the stencil in the
desired place.


4. Then spray over the stencil trying not to
smudge the paint underneath.


5. Just wait for it to dry !
